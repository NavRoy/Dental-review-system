How it works

  1. Staff scans the QR code at reception
  2. They pick a review template from the list
  3. Tap "Copy & Post" — text is copied, Google opens
  4. They paste and submit on Google
  5. That review is removed from the pool automatically

🤖 Update Keywords → Regenerate Reviews

Enter keywords that describe your clinic. The AI will generate 20 fresh review templates based on these. All current PENDING reviews will be replaced with new ones.

Include: specialties, location, what makes you different, treatments you offer
Default: Treatment Quality, Doctor & Staff, Cleanliness & Comfort, Overall Experience
